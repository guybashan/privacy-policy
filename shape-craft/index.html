Privacy Policy – Shape Craft: Tap Away 3D

Last updated: 13 July 2026

This privacy policy applies to the mobile game Shape Craft: Tap Away 3D ("the game") and describes how we handle your information.

Data we collect and why

  • Gameplay analytics: The game collects anonymous gameplay events (such as level starts, completions, and failures, session length, and game version) together with a randomly generated player identifier and basic device information (platform and OS version). This data is used only to understand how the game is played and to improve level design and stability.
  • No personal information: The game does not ask for or collect your name, email address, contacts, photos, location, or any other personal information. There are no user accounts.

Analytics provider

Gameplay events are processed by GameAnalytics, a service provider that processes this data on our behalf. Data is transmitted over an encrypted connection (HTTPS).

Data sharing

We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with third parties, except for the analytics service provider described above, which processes it on our behalf, or if required by law.

Data on your device

Your game progress (current level, gems, unlocked skins) is stored locally on your device only, and is deleted when you uninstall the game.

Children

The game is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Data retention and deletion

Analytics data is retained by our analytics provider in aggregate form. Because the player identifier is randomly generated and not linked to your identity, we cannot associate stored analytics data with a specific person. Uninstalling the game stops all data collection.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
