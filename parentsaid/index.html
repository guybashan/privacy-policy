Parents Aid Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

Parents Aid ("the app") is a parent-operated tool for routines, habits, learning, and reward coupons. This policy explains what data the app handles.

Summary

  • We do not require an account and we do not collect your name, email, or your child's name on our servers.
  • All progress (stars, punch cards, routines, rewards, child profiles) is stored only on your device.
  • We do not sell data or show ads.

Information stored on your device

  • Child profiles — names, ages, and settings you enter.
  • Activity data — routines, punch cards, quizzes, rewards, and growth stats.
  • Parent PIN — stored locally to gate parent-only settings.
  • Notifications — if enabled, routine reminders are scheduled locally on your device.

What we do not do

  • We do not upload your family's data to our servers.
  • We do not track children across apps or websites.
  • We do not require children to use the app directly — it is designed for parent-held devices.

Third-party services

  • Google Play — distributes the app and may process standard store analytics. See Google's privacy policy.
  • Fonts — Nunito is bundled inside the app; no font data is sent to third parties at runtime.

Data deletion

Uninstalling the app removes locally stored data from your device. You can also remove individual child profiles inside the app.

Children

Parents Aid is designed for parents to use with their children. The app is not intended for unsupervised use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children on our servers because data stays on the parent's device.

Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
